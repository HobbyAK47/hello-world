# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, writing code, and interacting in discussions.

Our standards
- Be respectful and kind. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and seek to resolve disagreements politely.

Reporting
If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, please report it to the repository maintainers by opening an issue titled "Code of Conduct report" or by contacting the repository owners on GitHub.

Enforcement
The maintainers may remove or modify content that violates this Code of Conduct and may temporarily or permanently ban participants who engage in unacceptable behavior.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant. For a full version or to adopt a different policy, consider visiting https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
